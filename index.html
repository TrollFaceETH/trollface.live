$TROLLFACE

One and only true King of Memes.

Troll Face is the ultimate meme coin on the Ethereum blockchain. It's built for fairness, with all its liquidity locked into the Uniswap V2 Router contract. This makes the coin always available and keeps its price stable.

Troll Face doesn't have an owner, which means that no single person or entity can make decisions or changes to the coin. Everyone is welcome to join the Troll Face community, and there are no blacklists.

Noone can mint more Troll Face tokens, so the ones that are out there can't be devalued by a sudden increase in supply. This scarcity makes each token potentially more valuable over time.

Troll Face is deflationary, meaning its supply naturally goes down over time. Because all its liquidity is locked in the Uniswap V2 router contract, trading commissions are also unreachable. This means that with every trade, the number of tokens in circulation decreases, which can increase the value of the remaining tokens.

Simply put, Troll Face is a meme coin done right. It's fair, transparent, and built for long-term growth. Jump in and be part of the fun!
